About Wuthering Waves

The Story

In the aftermath of the Lament, humanity struggles to survive in a transformed world. As a Rover awakening with no memory, you possess unique abilities to resonate with the frequencies of this new reality and fight against the Tacet Discords.

Open World

Explore vast landscapes filled with mysteries, secrets, and breathtaking vistas. Use parkour abilities to traverse cliffs, glide through valleys, and discover hidden locations throughout Solaris-3.

Combat System

Experience fast-paced, action-packed combat with fluid dodging, parrying, and devastating combo attacks. Master each Resonator's unique abilities and create your perfect team composition.
