Frequently Asked Questions

Is Wuthering Waves free to play?

Yes! Wuthering Waves is completely free to download and play with optional in-game purchases.

How much storage space do I need?

PC requires 30GB, mobile devices need 10GB, and PlayStation 5 needs 35GB of free storage space.

Can I play offline?

An internet connection is required to play Wuthering Waves as it's an always-online game.

Are there regional restrictions?

Wuthering Waves is available globally with support for multiple languages and regional servers.

How do I link my account?

Go to Settings > Account in-game and link your email or social media accounts for cross-save functionality.

What controller support is available?

PC version supports Xbox, PlayStation, and most USB controllers. Mobile has native touch controls.
